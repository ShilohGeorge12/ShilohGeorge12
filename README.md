### 👨‍💻 About Me

I'm a Full Stack Developer based in Lagos, Nigeria, with a BSc in Computer Science from Caleb University. I build performant, scalable web applications end-to-end — from polished React and Next.js frontends to robust Express.js and NestJS backends backed by MongoDB.

I'm the kind of developer who likes to understand how things work at every layer — not just get them working. That curiosity drives me to write cleaner code, architect better systems, and continuously improve.

Currently working at **Sleeky Programmers LTD**, shipping production-grade applications for clients across diverse industries.

- 🔭 Currently working with **Next.js, NestJS, TypeScript**
- 🌱 Exploring **React Native** and **Fintech integrations** (Payment Gateways, KYC APIs)
- 💬 Ask me about **React, Next.js, Express.js, NestJS, MongoDB**
- ⚡ Outside of code: hybrid athlete (lifting + running), anime lover, and a big believer in discipline and realistic, consistent growth
